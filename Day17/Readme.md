Salesforce Summer Program – Day 17

1. Agentforce Summary

Agentforce is Salesforce’s AI-powered platform for building intelligent agents that can automate tasks, answer questions, and assist users in enterprise systems.

Agentforce combines:

- AI reasoning
- Automation
- Flows
- Apex
- Data access
- Business logic

AI agents can understand user requests, retrieve data, trigger workflows, and perform actions automatically.

Agentforce represents the future of intelligent enterprise software systems.

3. AI Workflow Explanation

Workflow

User asks a question →

AI Agent receives the request →

Flow or Apex processes business logic →

Database retrieves required records →

AI generates response →

Action is executed automatically.

---

Example

Student asks:
"Am I eligible for scholarship?"

AI Agent

Understands the request.

Flow/Apex

Checks attendance, grades, and eligibility rules.

Database

Retrieves student records.

Response Generation

AI creates personalized answer.

Action Execution

Scholarship recommendation or notification is generated.

This shows how AI integrates with enterprise workflows and backend systems.

4. Risks of Enterprise AI

Hallucinations

AI may generate incorrect or false information.

---

Wrong Automation

AI may trigger incorrect actions due to misunderstanding.

---

Privacy Risks

Sensitive student or business data may be exposed.

---

Bias

AI decisions may become unfair or biased.

---

Incorrect Approvals

AI may approve or reject requests incorrectly.

---

Over-Automation

Too much automation may reduce human control.

---

Why Enterprises Must Control AI Systems

Enterprise AI systems require:

- Human supervision
- Security controls
- Validation checks
- Approval workflows
- Ethical governance

AI should assist decision-making, not replace responsible human oversight completely.

5. Reflection

AI agents may significantly change enterprise software development in the next 5 years.

Future systems may:

- Automate repetitive tasks
- Provide intelligent recommendations
- Interact using natural language
- Reduce manual operations
- Improve productivity and customer support

Developers may shift from only writing code to:

- Designing AI workflows
- Managing AI behavior
- Integrating AI with enterprise systems
- Ensuring security and ethical usage

AI will become an important part of modern enterprise software engineering, but human control and governance will remain essential.
